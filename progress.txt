## Codebase Patterns
- Projects CSV has 759 total rows; 685 with status "publish" are the ones to judge
- CSV requires `csv.field_size_limit(sys.maxsize)` due to large fields
- Each batch story covers 10 projects; judgments output to `judgments/<story_id>.json`
- Judging criteria: innovation, execution, impact, presentation, agentUse (each 1-10)
- Project data fields: id, name, description, deployed_url, repo_url, video_url, conversation_log, submission_metadata (JSON with model, tools, skills, commitCount, agentHarness, agentFramework, etc.)
- Always parse submission_metadata as JSON for structured tool/framework info
